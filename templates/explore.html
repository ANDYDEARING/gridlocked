Explore

Here the player explores the map, finding procedurally generated equipment and facing procedurally generated "Freelancers."

Freelancers can be captured by GridLocking them: surrounding them on all 4 sides and surviving for a full turn cycle. In practice, this is best done by destroying the remaining mechs and targeting the lone survivor for capture and ultimate recruitment.
