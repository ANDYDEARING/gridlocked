Battles

Here the player manages their current battles. Battles are not timed and are paused by default, encouraging players to play here and there when they have time.

PvE

In a typical PvE battle, the player faces procedurally generated "Freelancers."

Freelancers can be captured by GridLocking them: surrounding them on all 4 sides and keeping the surrounding mechs operational for a full turn cycle. In practice, this is best done by destroying the remaining mechs and targeting the lone survivor for capture and ultimate recruitment.

Other than recruitment, players can find randomly generated equipment.

PvP

In a typical PvP battle, players take turns and are alerted via notification when it is their turn.

PvP battles can either be Locked (where mechs are level balanced) or Open (where they carry their native stats).

Ranked Battles will involve constructing teams in the workshop from a pool of mechs and equipment identical for all other players.

Junkyard is also available with a common mech and equipment pool drafted by both players.

Rewards will vary based on the format, but could include xp, fixed rewards, or keeping drafted mechs or equipment.
